july 30, 2024 @ 11:47

i want a long wheelbase volkswagen id buzz. i don't normally like european cars that much, but vw buses are an exception. a new bus? and it's entirely electric? hell. yes.

it's a seven seater minivan with massive sliding doors and an electrochromatic sunroof. good lord it's magnificent. i absolutely adore it's two tone design. volkswagen was COOKING with this.

plus you can remove the third row and have a massive cargo area. it is an absolutely impeccable road tripping vehicle.

my god, in 10 years when they're affordable in the used market i'm getting one for sure. mark my words.
